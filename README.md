# Sales-Performance-Analysis
Which products, regions, and customer segments drive revenue, and which underperform?

Dataset: Superstore sales dataset (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohitsahoo/sales-forecasting/data)

Tools:
Pandas, Matplolib

- West region has the highest total revenue
- Conusmer segment dominates sales in all regions
- Revenue is concentrated in a small number of products
- Technology category otperforms others
- Seasonality with higher sales toward year-end

Recommendations:
- Prioritize high-performing regions and customer segments
- Focus marketing and inventory planning on top sub-categories
- Review underperforming products for pricing or assortment optimization
- Prepare operations for seasonal demand peaks
